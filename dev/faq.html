  • What saize are the miniatures?

    The majority of the minis are 35mm from base to eye level.

    The Icarus and Jaeger GEPS are 40mm from base to eye level.

    The Solomen and The Preacher are 45mm from base to eye level.

    The Titan, The Primus, The Olympian, & the Exloader are 60mm from base to eye level.

  • What are the miniatures made of?

    All our miniatures are made from high quality PVC (plastic).

  • Do the miniatures require assembly?

    All minis are pre-assembled so there is no need to spend hours constructing them and sticking your fingers together with super glue. :)

  • Why should I buy an expansion?

    All expansion packs come with a slew of new abilities, classes, boards, enemies, and missions that allow for much more replayability with the base game.

  • WHat is the size of the game tiles?

    25cm x 25cm. You will recieve 12 double-sided tiles.
